The weekend is open for attendees to present on all kinds of topics in unconference style. Presentations are usually 20-30 minutes in length, but in some cases much longer. Different styles include traditional slide accompanied talks, live coding demos, show and tell, mini workshops or guided group discussions. Time slots should be open at the camp, but filling out this form allows us to make sure there is sufficent time and space for your talk, as well as letting other attendees know about it ahead of time.
