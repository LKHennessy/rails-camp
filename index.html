About #RailsCampAdl

Rails Camp is a full weekend retreat for people interested in web development to gather and be awesome together. People attend for a variety of reasons: socialising, hacking, learning, teaching, drinking, gaming, and more. Though there is a focus on Ruby, we welcome anyone interested in programming or web technologies to join us.

    Friday

  • Buses to campsite, welcome talk, dinner, socialising

    Saturday

  • Talks, hacking, socialising

    Sunday

  • Talks, Ruby Australia meeting, hacking, socialising, demos

    Monday

  • Buses to CBD and airport, optional lunch

This camp will be held at Mylor Adventure Camp in the stunning Adelaide Hills from the 8th to the 11th of July.

Please see our Ticket section for pricing, FAQ and What to Bring sections for more information or Contact Us if you have any questions.

FAQ

What time should my flights be?

  • To coordinate with our buses, we suggest you land no later than 1pm Friday and depart no earlier than 1pm Monday, or 5pm if you are coming to lunch.

    • I plan to drive to the campsite, what time should I arrive?

  • Please arrive no earlier than 4pm on Friday, you can arrive as late as you like but keep in touch as best you can if you are arriving very late.

    • Will there be internet access at the camp?

  • The campground has pockets of 3g depending on your carrier, some of which are inside, which is handy because rain. It's probably best to assume you won't have internet access and plan accordingly.

    • How will I hack without the internet?!

  • We will provide a server with libraries and docs. If you need to Stack Overflow something, ask the person next to you!

    • I'm under 18, can I come? (or: I have kids who are under 18, can they come?)

  • Unfortunately we can't accomodate minors, please look into meetups and workshops in your area.

    • I have complicated dietary requirements, will you be able to feed me?

  • We are committed to providing food and drinks to accomodate all attendees needs, just let us know your requirements during ticket purchase and we will make sure your are catered for. If you have severe allergies please include this information and bring along your EpiPen.

    • How do I sign up for the Monday lunch or activity course if I already have my ticket?

  • Just email us with your name and ticket code and we will add you, assuming there is space!

    • I am signed up for the Monday lunch or activity course and I no longer wish to attend do I need to cancel my upgrade?

  • Yes, please email us with your name and ticket code so we can free up a spot for someone else.
